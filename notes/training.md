Good run
- Train loss decreases steadily -> model is learning patterns well
- Train loss very low -> model fits training data well
- Small gap between train and validation

Overfitting
- Validation loss stops improving or increases
- Gap between train and validation widens

Underfitting
- Train loss barely decreases
- Validation loss barely decreases

	•	Train ↓, Valid ↓ - learning + generalising (ideal)
	•	Train ↓, Valid ↑ - overfitting
	•	Train ≈ Valid, both high - underfitting
